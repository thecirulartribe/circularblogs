Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2024-12-22

Welcome to CircularBlogs! This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website to enhance your experience and comply with legal regulations.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files placed on your device to improve site performance, analyze user behavior, and provide personalized experiences. Some cookies are essential, while others help us understand how users interact with our site.

Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the proper functioning of our website, including:

  • CSRF Token Cookies: To secure form submissions and protect against cross-site request forgery.

2. Performance Cookies

These cookies collect anonymized data about user interactions to improve our website. For example:

  • Google Analytics: Tracks user behavior and site usage.
  • Microsoft Clarity: Provides insights into user interactions and heatmaps.

3. Functional Cookies

Enable features like remembering user preferences and improving the browsing experience.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services like Google Tag Manager to manage cookies and provide analytics. These third-party cookies are governed by their respective privacy policies.

Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may impact site functionality.
For detailed instructions, visit:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge

How We Use Cookies?

Cookies on our site help to:

  • Ensure secure form submissions (CSRF tokens).
  • Track site performance and user interactions (via Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity).
  • Personalize your browsing experience.

We do not store personal or sensitive data in cookies.

Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You can change your preferences at any time through the cookie settings or your browser.

Third-Party Services

We utilize the following tools:

  • Google Analytics: Learn more at Google Privacy Policy.
  • Microsoft Clarity: Learn more at Microsoft Privacy Statement.
